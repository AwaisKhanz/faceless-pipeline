Background music goes here.

Drop .mp3 or .wav files into this folder and pick one in the studio's Build
panel. Tracks are not committed to git — they are licensed to you and are not
necessarily yours to redistribute.

Free sources that allow YouTube monetisation:
    YouTube Audio Library   https://studio.youtube.com  (Audio Library)
    Pixabay Music           https://pixabay.com/music/
    Free Music Archive      https://freemusicarchive.org/  (check each licence)
