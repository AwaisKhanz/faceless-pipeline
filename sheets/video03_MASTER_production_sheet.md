### ⚠️ WHY 115 AND NOT MORE — the audio-clip ceiling
Google Vids caps the number of audio clips in a single video. At 149 scenes it threw **"Your video has reached the maximum number of audio clips"** and several scenes at the end silently failed to generate. Background music counts as an audio clip too, so the count has to leave room. **115 is the working number. Do not split scenes further.**

### ⭐ THE RULE
**One scene = one narration line = one media asset.**
- Never place a second asset on a scene that already has one — Vids makes it a picture-in-picture overlay, not a replacement. To swap: select the image on the canvas, delete it, then place the new one.

### 🔁 BUILD ORDER — 20 at a time
1. Enter narration for **20 scenes** (Voiceover → *Current scene* → type → **›** next-scene arrow).
2. **Pause ~0.6s after typing before clicking ›** — clicking too fast makes the arrow miss, and the next entry overwrites the previous scene.
3. Switch to *All scenes* → **Insert all voiceovers** → confirm those 20 generated.
4. Only then start the next 20.
5. Verify the scene counter after each block (should advance by exactly 20).

### Media
- **IMAGE scenes auto-size to the voiceover — no trimming.** That's why 103 of 115 are images.
- **VIDEO scenes must be trimmed:** playhead to voice end → `Cmd+Shift+S` → right-click trailing scene → Delete.
- **Stock search: use concrete photographic queries** ("alarm clock glowing in dark bedroom night"). Abstract queries ("many clocks pattern") return cartoon clip-art.

### Visual style
- Subjects mostly **60–75**, warm and real. Soft lamp light at night, warm daylight by day.
- **Recurring:** ONE woman ~65 as **Susan** (S29–S34, S70–S72, S82–S84); the **clock** motif; the **water/surfacing** motif; consistent **Cause 1–6 title cards**.
- ⚠️ **Break up the darkness** — alternate night shots with water metaphors, diagrams and daylight. Never more than ~2 dark-bedroom scenes in a row.
