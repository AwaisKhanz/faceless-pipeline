### ⚠️ WHY 115 AND NOT MORE — the audio-clip ceiling
Google Vids caps the number of audio clips in a single video. On Video 03, 149 scenes threw **"Your video has reached the maximum number of audio clips"** and several end scenes silently failed to generate. Background music counts as an audio clip too, so the count has to leave room. **115 is the working number. Do not split scenes further.**

### ⭐ THE RULE
**One scene = one narration line = one media asset.**
- Never place a second asset on a scene that already has one — Vids makes it a picture-in-picture overlay, not a replacement. To swap: select the image on the canvas, delete it, then place the new one.

### 🔁 BUILD ORDER — 20 at a time
1. Enter narration for **20 scenes** (Voiceover → *Current scene* → type → **›** next-scene arrow).
2. **Pause ~1.2s after typing before clicking ›** — clicking too fast makes the arrow miss, and the next entry overwrites the previous scene.
3. Go back to the start of the block and use **per-scene Insert voiceover**, waiting a few seconds between each. **Do NOT use "Insert/Update all voiceovers"** — it regenerates scenes that are already done.
4. Verify the scene counter after each block (should advance by exactly 20).
5. **Voice resets to Nyla on every new scene** — re-select **Persuader** before generating.

### Media
- **IMAGE scenes auto-size to the voiceover — no trimming.** That's why 103 of 115 are images.
- **VIDEO scenes must be trimmed:** playhead to voice end → `Cmd+Shift+S` → right-click trailing scene → Delete.
- **Stock search: use concrete photographic queries** ("older woman teaching piano to a young student"). Abstract queries return cartoon clip-art.

### Visual style
- Subjects mostly **65–85**, warm and real. Natural daylight, kitchens, gardens, cafés, living rooms. **This video is bright** — it's the opposite of Video 03's night palette.
- **Recurring:** ONE man ~68 as **Arthur** (S27–S32, S72–S76, S88); ONE woman ~84 as **the piano teacher** (S1–S3, S61, S75); the **hand of cards** motif; the **house with many doors** motif; the **thermostat dial** motif.
- ⚠️ **Alternate faces with metaphor shots** so it never becomes a slideshow of portraits. Never more than ~2 solo-portrait scenes in a row.
